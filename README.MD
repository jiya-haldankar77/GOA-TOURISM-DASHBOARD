### **1. Tourist Type (Domestic vs International)**
- Shows the trends of domestic and international tourists across years and months.
- Helps understand seasonal spikes and travel preferences.

### **2. Region-wise Tourism**
- Compares **North Goa vs South Goa** tourism counts.
- Month-wise distribution reveals regional tourism flow.

### **3. Transport Mode Share**
- Breakdown of tourists using **Air, Road, and Rail**.
- Useful for transport capacity planning and infrastructure analysis.

### **4. Accommodation Spend Trends**
- Shows how tourists spend across:
  - Hotels  
  - Hostels  
  - Homestays  
  - Resorts  
  - Airbnb  
  - Guest Houses

- Trends in usage of:
  - Online platforms  
  - Travel agents  
  - Offline bookings  

### **6. Tourism Pressure Map**
- State-wise distribution of incoming tourists.
- Highlights top contributing states.
